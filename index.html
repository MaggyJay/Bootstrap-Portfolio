About Me


My name is Magdalene James and I’m training to become a full-stack web-developer. Ever since I was a young girl I’ve been interested in tech. In 2nd grade my favorite class was computer lab and from there I’ve been on my own path of self-discovery for how tech fits into my life. As a tween during the MySpace and IMVU days I was working with HTML/CSS without even realizing it.

My love for technology drove me to major in Information Science and minor in Information Assurance during my undergrad studies. I learned many branches of technology from IT Project Management, to Java, too a little of web development. I’m now in a place where I need some sort of creative outlet as well as a challenge to really push myself in becoming a web-developer. I am looking forward to this chapter in my life!
